About the Project

Smart Student Management System is an intelligent, web-based platform designed to simplify and modernize academic administration. It centralizes student data, attendance, and institutional processes into a secure and user-friendly system. By integrating automation and future-ready AI capabilities, the platform reduces manual effort, improves accuracy, and enhances transparency for students, faculty, and administrators. Built with scalability and performance in mind, it aims to create a smarter, more efficient campus ecosystem.
